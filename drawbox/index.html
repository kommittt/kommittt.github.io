where am i.txt

a drawbox is a place where you can draw anything you want! show off your art skills :)
as long as you follow the rules of course, which are written below

once you're done, just click the submit button! leave your username if you want to make yourself known, but you can leave it as blank (it will autofill as anonymous)

i occassionally drop comments as well :)

if you want to leave a written message, head on over to my guestbook instead!


notes:
best viewed on desktop! mobile view is a little broken (canvas offset)
please make sure you are not zoomed in, the windows might hide the submit button
