welcome! this is a place where i (and a few others!!) occasionally add more sounds for any of beepbox's forks that allow custom sample loading, which is ultraabox, abyssbox, slarmoo's, and etc (theres a LOT) however i named this after ultrabox because 1. i cant stop thinking about ultrakill whenever i hear it and 2. when i made this i didnt know about the others

how do i use this? navigate to a category you like or go to all-samples using the sidebar, look for a specific sound you want by clicking the play button or using the search bar, and click on the copy to clipboard button to copy the filegarden link! after that, just paste it :)
what about presets? no clue when, but it is a plan that i have
