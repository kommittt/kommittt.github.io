do i have to link every time i use this nope! share it if you want, or keep it to yourself. the more people who know about this, the better though (i think)
how often do you update the site? haha yeah
how do i submit dropbox samples? when you share a link to dropbox, it isnt in a format ultrabox supports: https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/dlgst8y93ef0khe863tkx/us-eas-alert-sound.wav?rlkey=llr2umg5gkkafw2gsu16lz7xz&st=4jrs57sf&dl=0 what you need to do is replace dropbox.com to dl.dropboxusercontent.com and REMOVE the &st=.....&dl=0 parts. why? &st=... is a share token, or a TRACKER. remove that shit. &dl is basically just download thing, not needed here.
why do new categories take longer to add and not instant (like the user-submitted one)? while its true that google sheets now handles the data, i still have to make a "ui" for everyone to see it in the site - which are the pages you are looking at. i have to add an html, edit the js so it accepts it as a new category, make a new card in the mainpage as well as make it a link on the sidebar, requiring me to push out an update to my page plus, i dont exactly check on this everyday...
how do i help out/id like to share my samples if you have a filegarden with your own samples (like a category you wish to add) and would like to share it to everyone here, use this: contribution google form
can i request something? yes, but it will take longer to add due to the extra work. if you have a soundfont you want extracted, head on over to this link: requests google form
